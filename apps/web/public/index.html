Emergency Recovery Loop

Emergency mode is for outages and disaster conditions. It creates review evidence, not a blockchain transfer.

Offline IOU
No transfer

pyra_emergency_iou records preserve who owes whom, the PYRA amount, the intended chain, and the reason for later settlement.

Two-Party Handoff
QR copies

Debtor and creditor payloads carry the same IOU id so both parties have a chance to preserve the obligation when connectivity returns.

Admin Review
